About

Engineering-first. Business-minded.

Ewhothera Samuel founded D RANKY Studio to close the gap between what businesses need technically and what they're able to build on their own. The studio works at the intersection of software engineering and applied AI — building systems that are reliable in production, not just impressive in a demo.

Every project is approached the same way: understand the business goal first, then choose the simplest technology that reliably gets there. That discipline is what lets D RANKY Studio move fast without leaving clients with software they can't maintain.

SELF-INITIATED / FOUNDER-LED

Building a full-stack storefront for D RANKY Luxury Fashion

Solodesign + build
End-to-endauth to payment

Problem

Off-the-shelf storefront builders couldn't support the account flow, order tracking, and payment experience the brand needed without heavy workarounds and recurring platform fees.

Approach

Designed and built the storefront from scratch — customer accounts, cart and checkout, delivery details capture, and Paystack-powered payments — with collections organized by price tier and a dedicated order-tracking flow, all handled solo.

Outcome

A live, working storefront covering the full customer journey from browsing to paid, tracked order — proof of the same full-stack process applied to client engagements. View the live site →

CLIENT: MULTI-LOCATION RETAIL BUSINESS

Reducing stockouts with a demand-forecasting model

32%fewer stockouts
6 wksbuild time

Problem

Manual, gut-feel stock ordering was leading to frequent stockouts on high-demand items and excess inventory on slow movers.

Approach

Trained a forecasting model on two years of historical sales and seasonality data, then integrated its recommendations directly into the existing ordering workflow.

Outcome

Stockouts on top-selling items dropped by roughly a third within the first full quarter of use, without adding headcount.
